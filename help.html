Where to Get Help?

When seeking help for depression the first place you should start is your local docter/GP, they will do an assessment to see the severity of your condition, they will then do a mental health care plan & refer you to a local therapist. They can also do a medication option and provide a script for this, in which case you would go to your local Pharmacy/Drug Store and get the medication.
